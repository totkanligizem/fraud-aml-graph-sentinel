====================================================================
R1) Data licensing ve data handling riski
- Cozum: data registry + licence manifest + no-raw-data policy gate

R2) Point-in-time leakage riski
- Cozum: feature-time kural testleri + dbt generic/singular testler

- Cozum: ranking katmani + queue-level KPI

- Cozum: retrieval sandbox + redaction + strict tool allowlist

R6) Reproducibility kaybi riski
- Cozum: pinned dependencies + deterministic seeds + artifact lineage
